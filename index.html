I have spent the past three years looking into Eyebrights, or Euphrasia. Specifically I am interested in whether these parasitic plants are more specialised than we think, and to understand the process of hybridisation better in the genus.

This website aims to track some of the things I find interesting. Find out more about me here. Visit the source code for this website and other projects here and enjoy!
