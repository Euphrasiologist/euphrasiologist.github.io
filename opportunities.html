Opportunities

Unfortunately ARU is not part of a DTP currently, but this may change soon. We have competitive budgets for a limited number of PhD students however. If PhD's are advertised, they will always be on findaPhD.com.

General opportunities

I would be glad to be contacted from anyone who is interested in plant ecology, genomics, or the intersection of these topics. I am happy to try and advise for sources of funding, and potentially write grants with keen prospective students, should the opportunity arise.

Collaborations

I am always open to collaborations with folk, and I'm grateful to the many collaborators who have worked with me over the years.
