Hybridisation in Saxifraga

[link—standalone] A recreation of a cool little graph I saw in Saxifrages of Europe [1], showing the extent of hybridisation between the subsections of the genus Saxifraga. Filled circles indicate within subsection hybridisation reported, and dashed lines are uncertian hybrid reports.
