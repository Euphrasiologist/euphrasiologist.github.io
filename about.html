About

This proto-website is the outreach for some of the work I have been doing, why I think it's interesting, and how you could do the same thing too! I'm a firm believer in data, code and workflow transparency. Github is an incredible tool for this, as well as learning to code at the same time.

I am a postgraduate researcher at the University of Edinburgh, working on plants in the British Isles called Eyebrights (or Euphrasia). I use common garden experiments, field observations, statistics and genomics to understand how hybrid species might evolve in Euphrasia.

I am particularly interested in botany, plant life history, aliens and invasives, hybridisation and coding (R, python, awk, BASH)

I'd love to hear from anyone interested in similar projects and am always open to collaborations, please email me at: max.brown@ed.ac.uk
